Rules and Regulation of our College


  • Students must obey the rules and regulations that are carried by the College Chancellors.
  • In all working days uniform is Compulsory.
  • It is compulsory to wear uniform in all programs related with College.
  • Student must wear uniform with collar and below hand knee.
  • Don’t wear Ornaments inside the College.
  • Inside the class don’t use Electronic things.
  • Inside the campus politics activities are not Allowed.
  • Students must wear proper uniform along with ID card while attending the classes.
  • All Programs are conduct only while Principal sir permission.
  • Students should show Respect to the Teaching staff, College and non Teaching staff.
  • All Programs are conduct only while Principal sir permission.
  • Students will have to obey the Rules and Regulations of the college. Principal’s decision will be final in any academic or disciplinary matters.
  • Students must attend all the exams conducted by the College.
  • On time students must come to the class.
  • College fees must be pay on time otherwise fine will be collected.
  • The fees once paid will not be refunded even if the student for any reasons shall leave the institution after Admission.
  • On class time don’t go outside without Principal sir permission.
  • Parents must participate the Parents meeting.
  • According to ‘Prohibition Of Raging’ act in Kerala on 1998 that is offense in College.
  • Students are responsible for keeping the Classroom and the things related with classroom neat and clean.
  • Ragging is strictly Prohibited.
