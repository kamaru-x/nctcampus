About us

Welcome National College

National college was established in 1975 for imparting tuition classes for school and college going students. The classes started in a small rented building with a few students and five teachers who were the promoters. Mr M.V. Purushothaman was the founder Principal and still continues in that post.

In 1977 the 1 st batch of Pre-Degree(commerce & humanities) of Calicut University started. The support of the general public was very encouraging. The whole hearted support of the parents coupled with the persevarance and hardwork of the teachers produced excellent results and the college became popular within a few years.

Principle's Message

Principal, Shri Purushothaman M V is a native of Taliparamba. He alongwith a handful educational visionaries of Kannur District established the college in 1975. He is the founder Principal of the college and for the last 46 years he has meticulously managed the college. The college is extremely proud that its rich and glorious tradition is due to the uncompromising discipline and professional ethics espoused by the Principal. He tried his best to promote scientific spirit, humanism and free thinking in students.

In 1996, he established NICT the Computer wing of National College. In 2002, he promoted Gurudev Educational Trust with its headquarters at Taliparamba. He is the founder chairman of Gurudev Arts & Science College (Mathil) affiliated to Kannur University.
